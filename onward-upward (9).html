The Framework

Onward is the call to action in leadership.

Onward & Upward is for leaders who are responsible for outcomes and big ideas, lead through change, growth, and innovation, and are ready to innovate without compromising values, people, or momentum.

Responsible for outcomes and big ideas
Leading through change, growth, and innovation
Ready to innovate without compromising values or momentum
O
Orient to Purpose
Lead with clarity through chaos.

Having a clear purpose sharpens decision-making and keeps teams moving in the same direction — even when conditions shift.

N
Nurture Trust
Trust is strategic.

Your teams move as a connected unit when trust is strong. It's not a soft skill — it's a leadership asset.

W
Well-Being
Sustainable leadership requires resilience.

Burnout doesn't just affect individuals — it stalls organizations. Well-being is a leadership strategy, not an afterthought.

A
Authentic
Your leadership voice should sound like you.

Authenticity isn't about being casual — it's about being true to who you are while meeting the demands of where you lead.

R
Results-Focused
Vision matters, but so do outcomes.

Great leaders don't lose sight of what they're building toward. Clarity of vision and accountability for results must coexist.

D
Development
Resilient leaders don't plateau.

They learn, pivot, and evolve. Leadership development is an ongoing investment — in yourself and in the people you lead.

With Onward as part of the leadership strategy, leaders can move forward with intention. That's preventative leadership instead of reactive leadership.

Why Go Onward & Upward

Dr. Amber K. Brooks

Physician  ·  Researcher  ·  Speaker  ·  Leadership Navigator

Dr. Amber K. Brooks is a physician, researcher, speaker, and leadership navigator who helps accomplished leaders lead with clarity, courage, and authenticity during moments of change.

With a background in medicine and organizational leadership, Dr. Brooks understands high-stakes environments where decisions matter, trust is essential, and uncertainty is constant. Her work focuses on helping leaders strengthen their leadership voice, foster collaboration and well-being, and remain results-focused while navigating complexity and transformation.

Through Onward & Upward, Dr. Brooks partners with leaders across healthcare, corporate, higher education, and mission-driven organizations who need perspective more than they need motivation. She brings a grounded, strategic, and human approach to leadership development, helping executives, faculty, and emerging student leaders move forward with intention — and lead in ways that are both effective and sustainable.
